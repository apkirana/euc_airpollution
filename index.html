About The Project

European Universities' Competition Entry

This project is an entry for the European Universities' Competition on Artificial Intelligence, focused on AI and Climate Change Solutions. Our mission is to leverage agentic AI to provide an autonomous, explainable, and scalable solution for air quality forecasting.

By focusing on the JABODETABEK region, we aim to create a powerful tool that can aid urban planning and improve public health outcomes in one of the world's most dynamic urban areas.
